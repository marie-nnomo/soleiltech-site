About Us

SoleilTech is a solar energy company based in Nantes, France. We specialize in the installation of high-quality solar panels for both residential and commercial buildings. Our mission is to make solar power accessible, affordable, and efficient for everyone.

Internships & Apprenticeships at SoleilTech

At SoleilTech, we believe in developing the next generation of renewable energy leaders. We offer various internship and apprenticeship opportunities for students passionate about the environment and solar technology. By joining us, you will gain hands-on experience working alongside experts in the field.

Internship Opportunities

  • Solar Panel Technician Internship (6-month duration)

    Mission: Assist our team in the installation, maintenance, and troubleshooting of solar panel systems for residential and commercial projects. Gain hands-on experience in solar energy technology and project management.

    Advantages: Opportunity to learn from experienced technicians, exposure to a variety of solar systems, and a chance to develop your technical skills.

    Profile Sought: Students from engineering schools, level B2 in English, team-oriented, strong communication skills, and a passion for renewable energy.

  • Project Management Assistant Internship (6-month duration)

    Mission: Support project managers in planning, organizing, and executing solar energy projects. Ensure projects are delivered on time and within budget while working with various teams.

    Advantages: Learn project management techniques, collaborate with engineers and technicians, and gain valuable experience in the solar energy industry.

    Profile Sought: Engineering or business students with a B2 level of English, excellent organizational skills, and an interest in sustainability and project management.

Apprenticeship Opportunities

  • Solar Energy Consultant Apprenticeship (1-year contract)

    Mission: Learn the ins and outs of solar energy solutions. Assist our consultants in assessing client needs, providing customized solutions, and supporting the application for government subsidies and incentives.

    Advantages: Work closely with experienced consultants, engage in client interactions, and help develop sustainable energy solutions.

    Profile Sought: Engineering students, level B2 in English, excellent communication skills, and a passion for renewable energy solutions.

  • Solar Panel Design Apprentice (1-year contract)

    Mission: Work alongside our engineering team to design solar panel systems tailored to client specifications. Contribute to the technical aspects of the design and implementation of solar installations.

    Advantages: Gain exposure to cutting-edge design tools, work on real-world projects, and deepen your understanding of solar energy systems.

    Profile Sought: Engineering students with strong technical skills, proficiency in design software, and a commitment to renewable energy.
